Community System Strengthening

Community-Led Monitoring

Project Brief

The Community Systems Strengthening (CSS) Project supports the National AIDS Control Programme (NACP) by institutionalising meaningful community participation in programme planning, implementation, monitoring and review. The project strengthens structured community platforms and mechanisms—such as Community Champions (CCs), State and District Community Resource Groups (S-CRGs and D-CRGs), Community-Led Monitoring (CLM), and State-Level Networks (SLNs)—to ensure community voices inform programme decisions and service delivery.

The project is implemented in coordination with NACO, SACS and DISHA/DAPCU units, with the Implementation Partner playing a facilitative and capacity-building role. Emphasis is placed on standardised processes, documentation, and monitoring systems to ensure consistency, accountability and sustainability of community engagement across states and districts.

Project Goal

To strengthen community systems within the National AIDS Control Programme by enabling structured, informed and sustained participation of communities in HIV programme planning, implementation, monitoring and accountability mechanisms.

Project Objectives

Engage Champions

To identify, train and engage Community Champions from key populations, PLHIV and vulnerable groups to support community participation and feedback mechanisms.

Strengthen CRGs

To strengthen the functioning of State and District Community Resource Groups as formal platforms for programme review, problem-solving and follow-up action.

Institutionalise CLM

To institutionalise Community-Led Monitoring to capture community experience with HIV services and ensure timely corrective actions through programme structures.

Support Networks (SLNs)

To support the formation or strengthening of State-Level Networks to function as stable, representative and compliant community platforms.

Standardised Systems

To establish standardised monitoring, documentation and reporting systems that track CSS activities, outputs and follow-up actions across all levels.

Implementation Plan

1. Community Champion Strengthening

Champions will be nominated, selected and trained using standardised processes. They will engage in CSS activities including CLM, CRG participation and outreach.

2. CRG Strengthening (State & District)

Existing S-CRGs and D-CRGs will be reviewed and reconstituted. Regular meetings will review feedback, CLM findings and programme issues.

3. Community-Led Monitoring

CLM will be rolled out using digital feedback mechanisms. Feedback will be reviewed through D-CRG meetings and follow-up actions monitored.

4. State-Level Network Strengthening

New SLNs will be formed where required, and existing networks strengthened through governance support, compliance, and capacity building.

5. Monitoring, Reporting and MIS

A standardised online MIS will be used to track all CSS processes, participation, outputs and follow-up actions across all levels.
